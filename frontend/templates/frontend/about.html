{% trans "About ODEON" %}

{% blocktrans %} ODEON is a centralized repository whose objective is to facilitate communication betweeen Open Government Data (OGD) stakeholders: citizens, developers and publishers.

As citizen, you can suggest a project idea for developers and also follow the progress of the project development if needed.
As developer, you can discover the projects that citizens are interested in and also get feedback from users during the development progress. In addition, you can request datasets from publishers and also report issues found in datasets used in your projects. You can also submit your reuses of OGD.
As publisher, you can discover the projects that used your published datasets. You can also find out about requested data and reported issues with your published datasets. {% endblocktrans %}
