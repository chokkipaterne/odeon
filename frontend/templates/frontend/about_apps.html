{% trans "About Apps" %}

{% blocktrans %} The Apps (ODEON, CitizenApps, ODE) aim at providing technical solutions to reduce barriers to Data Storytelling by OGD stakeholders, with a particular focus on the following barriers: B1) lack of awareness of the existence and usefulness of OGD (ODEON, CitizenApps), B2) lack of communication between OGD stakeholders (ODEON) and B3) difficulty of turning OGD into stories (ODE).

ODEON (Open Data Exchange solutiON) is an exchange platform whose objective is to facilitate communication betweeen Open Government Data (OGD) stakeholders: citizens, developers and publishers.
CitizenApps is a mobile and minimal version of ODEON that aims to help citizens to access in one place all OGD reuses in their area.
ODE (Open Data Explorer) helps citizens, developers and publishers to turn their open dataset into stories (mainly dashboards). {% endblocktrans %}
